📋 Connect Your Google Sheet

Your sheet needs to be published publicly so the app can read it. Follow these steps:

  1. Open your Google Sheet → click File in the menu
  2. Go to Share → Publish to web
  3. In the first dropdown select Sheet1 (or your sheet name)
  4. In the second dropdown select Comma-separated values (.csv)
  5. Click Publish → confirm → copy the URL
  6. Paste the URL in the code replacing SHEET_URL at the top of the script, then hit Sync
⚠ Artwork not showing? Google Drive image links are blocked by browsers due to CORS restrictions. Use a free image host instead: upload your artwork to Imgur (imgur.com/upload) or ImgBB (imgbb.com) and paste the direct image URL (ending in .jpg/.png) into the ART Work column. Google Drive links will never work as img src in a web app.
