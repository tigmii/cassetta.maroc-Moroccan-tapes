Get your Lark audio links — easy, no coding needed

📋 How to use
1. Open your Lark folder and click Share → Get public link (make sure it's public)
2. Come back here and paste your Lark App Token below
3. Click Load my files — all your audio files appear
4. Click Copy link next to each tape and paste it in your Google Sheet music column
📂 Open your Lark folder ↗
✅ Simplest approach — no token needed!
1. Open your Lark folder above
2. Right-click any file → Copy download link (or click ⋯ → Download, then copy the URL from your browser)
3. Paste that URL directly into the music column of your Google Sheet
4. The URL looks like: https://p1-xxx.byteimg.com/...
Or use the automatic method with a Lark token:
Get it from: open.larksuite.com/api-explorer → click "Get Token" → copy user_access_token
