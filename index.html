🔗 Connect Your Google Sheet

Paste your published CSV URL below to connect your data.

How to get your published URL
  1. Open your Google Sheet → click File in the top menu
  2. Click Share → then Publish to web
  3. In the first dropdown select your sheet tab name (e.g. Sheet1)
  4. In the second dropdown select Comma-separated values (.csv)
  5. Click the Publish button → confirm with OK
  6. Copy the URL shown — it starts with https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/e/2PACX-
  7. Paste it in the field above and click Connect
⚠️ The URL in your browser address bar will NOT work. You must use the URL from File → Share → Publish to web. It contains /e/2PACX- in it.
